About us

History of our hotel

The Jakubowy Hotel is a unique place in the very center of Gdynia, combining the charm of a historic building with elegant and modern design. Our hotel impresses with an extremely classic style, elegant finishes, and at the same time a warm and family atmosphere. It was created so, to meet the needs of both those who plan to spend their vacation by the sea, as well as for those coming to Gdynia on business!

Great location

Jakubowy hotel is located in the very center, near the famous Swietojanska Street and Seaside Boulevard.This is a very young city full of seagulls, ships and salt water.... A hundred years ago there were a dozen fishermen's huts and an alley leading to a wooden pier.
